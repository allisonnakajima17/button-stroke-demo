Button Stroke Treatment

Demonstrating two approaches for consistent button heights with strokes across Web, iOS, and Android platforms.

Fixed heights ensure both filled and outlined buttons are identical in size across all platforms. No invisible borders, padding compensation, or complex workarounds required.
